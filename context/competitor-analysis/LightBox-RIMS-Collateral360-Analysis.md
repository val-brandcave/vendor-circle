## Product Deep Dive: RIMS

### What is RIMS?
**RIMS (Real Estate Information Management System)** is a procurement and workflow management system connecting lenders with vendors for appraisal, valuation, environmental, construction, and flood services.

**Source:** https://www.lightboxre.com/product/rims-real-estate-information-management-system/

### Core Features

#### 1. **Streamlined Lending Workflow**
- Easily configurable to meet lending processes
- Role-based permissions
- Efficiency improvements â†’ increased loan volume capacity
- **Impact:** "Lenders using RIMS report efficiency improvements"

#### 2. **RIMSCentral (Vendor Portal)** â­ KEY FEATURE
- Vendors acknowledge assignments or submit bids
- Secure document collaboration
- Vendors upload reports and invoices
- Lenders upload assignment details
- **This is what we need parity with**

#### 3. **Reputable Catalog Partners**
- Built-in data and services from third-party sources
- AVMs (Automated Valuation Models)
- Evaluations
- Flood certifications
- Custom market information reports
- "Most respected names in the industry"

#### 4. **Automated Residential Lending**
- Auto-award capabilities
- Round-robin assignment
- Weighted assignment based on:
  - Geographic proximity
  - Experience
  - Rating
  - Price
- Automated appraisal report rules checking
- UAD compliance checking
- Fair Lending compliance checking

### LightBox Marketplace Integration
- Vendor-agnostic digital platform
- Connects lenders with vetted 3rd party vendors
- Non-procuring report services (evaluations, inspections)
- Seamless integration with RIMS
- Centralized vendor relationship management

## LightBox Fundamentals (New AI Feature)

### What It Is
**AI-powered appraisal intelligence** that transforms static appraisal documents into structured, searchable data.

### Key Features
- Delivered via Excel Add-In
- Eliminates manual data entry
- Reduces turnaround times
- Portfolio-level insights for credit, risk, compliance teams
- 80+ key fields and metrics extracted
- Custom AI + Heuristic Engine (trained on narrative appraisals)
- Zero setup needed

### Why This Matters
**LightBox is investing heavily in AI/automation.** This shows their roadmap and where the market is heading. Phase 2 consideration for us.
